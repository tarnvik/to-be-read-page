How to use

Add a book to the TBR list by using the text field. You can move it between the different lists by clicking on or draging the item you want to move. You can also drag them to change their order. To delete an item either drag it to the trash can or click on the trash can to activate "delete mode". When delete mode is on clicking on an item will remove it. To turn off deletemode simply click on the trash can again.
